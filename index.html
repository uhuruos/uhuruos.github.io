🔒 Why Privacy Matters

UhuruOs your digital life private by default. No telemetry, strong cryptographic defaults, and carefully chosen packages that respect user freedom.

Key Features

  • Based on stable Debian with timely security patches
  • Hardened defaults: firewall, kernel hardening, minimal attack surface
  • No telemetry; privacy tools preinstalled (Tor, VPN tooling, secure messaging)
  • Lightweight desktop & power-user friendly configuration
